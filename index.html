About Us
The place where you will feel indulged
Lincoln Barbershop has been open since summer 2020 and is one of the most modern barbershops in the country. With us you can find a wide range of services, the goal being to see you satisfied and fulfilled, from a moral and financial point of view. Come and spend your time in a pleasant and relaxed way, with the most positive barbers.
At Lincoln Barbershop you don't have time to be bored, xbox games, mafia, uno and much more.
Do not hesitate to try, come to give yourself the best haircut at an affordable price. Our team of barbers is waiting for you with the greatest love. We are located on 10/1 Kiev street, Rîșcani sector, and on 95 Alexandru cel Bun street, Centru sector, Chisinau.
